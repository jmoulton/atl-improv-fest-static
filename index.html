Celebrating the Atlanta Improv Community

The Village Theatre is playing host to the festival that brings Atlanta improvisers together, celebrating the growth of improv in our fine city. The Atlanta Improv Festival also opens its doors to performers from all over the country, making it a weekend full of laughing and learning from each other.

Not only does it plan to be a weekend full of great improv, but proceeds from ticket sales supports Dad’s Garage as they move into their new home. It is safe to say that the Atlanta Improv community wouldn’t be where it is without Dad’s around for the past 20 years, so we’re excited to bring everyone together to propel Atlanta Improv forward for years to come.

FAQs

How do I buy a pass to see shows?
Wristbands are on sale now! You can buy a weekend wristband for $30. That wristband will get you into every show during the festival.
If you are a performer, you can buy a wristband for $25. We’ll send performers info on the discounted wristbands after they are accepted for the weekend.
Please check the schedule for show hours.

Can I buy an individual show ticket?
Because the proceeds from the weekend passes are going to such a good cause, we are going to ask that anyone buy the full weekend pass no matter how long they plan on watching shows. You should stay all weekend anyway. Come on!

Getting there

The Village Theatre is located in Downtown Atlanta, in the Old Fourth Ward neighborhood. Conveniently located in the Pencil Factory Lofts, The Village Theatre has covered parking on- site, and the theatre is across the street from the King Memorial MARTA train station.
